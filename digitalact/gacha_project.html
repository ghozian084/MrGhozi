🎮 Project 1: Gacha Analyst

Math is the ultimate cheat code.

In this 1-day digital project, you will investigate "Loot Boxes" and "Gacha" mechanics to see if the game is fair or just a math trap.

Learning Goals: Percentage to Decimal, Fractions, Powers, and Algebraic Models.

Step 1: Data Hunting

Pick a game you play (Genshin, Roblox, MLBB, FC Mobile, etc.) and find the official Drop Rates.

  • Search online for: "[Game Name] official gacha rates" or "drop probabilities".
  • Find a specific Rare, Epic, or Legendary item.
  • Action: Take a screenshot of the item and its probability (%).
Example: "SSR Character" Drop Rate = 0.6%

Step 2: Basic Conversions

Convert your percentage (%) into three different formats:

  • Decimal: Divide the % by 100. (0.6 ÷ 100 = 0.006)
  • Fraction: Write it as a fraction and simplify. (6/1000 = 3/500)
  • Ratio: Calculate "1 in every X pulls". (1 ÷ 0.006 = 166.6)
Format: % → Decimal → Fraction → Ratio

Step 3: The Power of Probability

How likely are you to be "Unlucky"? Calculate the chance of getting NOTHING in 10 pulls.

  • Find the Lose Rate: 1 - Win Rate (Decimal).
  • Use Powers: (Lose Rate)10
Example: If Win Rate is 0.05, Lose Rate is 0.95.
Probability of losing 10 times = (0.95)10 ≈ 0.598 (59.8%)

Step 4: Algebraic Pity

Games usually have a "Pity System" (Guaranteed win after X pulls).

  • Define x as the price of 1 pull (Gems/Money).
  • Define y as the number of pulls until pity.
  • Create the model: C = x · y
Calculate the TOTAL cost to guarantee your item.
