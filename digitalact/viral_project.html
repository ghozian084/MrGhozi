📈 Project 6: The Viral Trend Forecaster

How do videos reach 10 Million views?

You are a Social Media Data Consultant. You will use Algebra, Powers, and Roots to predict the "Viral Curve" of a new trend.

$$\text{Reach} = \text{Initial} \times (\text{Rate})^{\text{Time}}$$

In this project, you will prove that small changes in the Rate create massive changes in Success.

Step 1: Forecasting with Powers ($x^n$)

Pick an Initial Seed (number of friends) and a Growth Rate (how many people each viewer shares with).

Example: 50 friends and a 3x sharing rate over 5 days:

$$50 \times 3^5 = 50 \times 243 = 12,150 \text{ Views}$$
  • Action: Calculate your Day 7 reach for your final report.

Step 2: Working Backward with Roots ($\sqrt[n]{x}$)

If you want to hit 1,000,000 views in exactly 3 days, what must the daily growth rate be?

$$\sqrt[3]{1,000,000} = 100$$

Conclusion: To hit a Million in 3 days, every viewer must share the post with 100 people.

Step 3: Engagement Mix (Fractions)

Calculate the "Interaction Ratio." If $\frac{2}{5}$ of viewers Like and $\frac{1}{10}$ Comment:

$$\frac{2}{5} + \frac{1}{10} = \frac{4}{10} + \frac{1}{10} = \frac{5}{10} = 50\%$$
